Collections of gene sets from the Molecular Signature Database provided by the [msigdbr](https://cran.r-project.org/web/packages/msigdbr/vignettes/msigdbr-intro.html) package can be utilized for a gene set enrichment analysis on the genes of interest. The abbreviations can be checked to see which gene set they stand for by clicking onto the *MSigDB Collections* button.
<br><center><img src="screenshot5-1.png" style="height: 477px; width:558.75px;"/></center>
<br>**Example**
<br>Hallmark gene sets, KEGG pathways, Reactome pathways, GO biological processes and oncogenic signature sets are selected among the collections.
<br><br>Besides from the genes involved in the most enriched GO biological processes, hallmark genes of early and late estrogen response such as *AQP3* and *CCN5* can be identified among the genes of interest.
<br><br>Gene sets can be investigated by typing down certain keywords of interest as well. For instance, genes that are involved in mineralocorticoid signaling among the selected genes are checked.
