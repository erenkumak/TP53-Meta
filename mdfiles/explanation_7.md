Plotting the genes of interest on their chromosomal positions along with their logFC values can provide insight into whether up/downregulated genes reside in close proximity. Vizualizations can be done by spanning all chromosomes or by specifying a chromosome or a chromosomal region of interest.
<br><br>Note that one of the initially selected experimental groups should be chosen for the logFC values to use.
