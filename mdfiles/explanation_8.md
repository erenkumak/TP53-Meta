Several genes among the gene list created in the *RNA-seq* part can be selected to plot broadPeak and/or BigWig files underneath the genomic regions of the genes. The distance to the genes of interest can be adjusted to extend or narrow down the plotted region of ChIP-seq signals. 
<br><br>The plotted genes will be colour-coded by their logFC values. The colour codes can be changed preferentially. Plotting ChIP-seq data together with the differentially expressed genes can allow for interpreting chromatin-level regulations across regions where the selected genes of interest are located.
<br><br>**Example**

There are also peak calls from the ChIP-seq data of DRSP+E2 treated T47D samples where *PGR*, which is significantly upregulated upon DRSP+E2 treatment but not upon DRSP treatment, is located.
